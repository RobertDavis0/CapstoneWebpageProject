About

Tee Print World Wide is a tee printing company that takes special orders and has pre-made tees. Here we make whatever shirt design you would like. We have a team of people who work on making the desgin as accurately as possible. We enjoy being able to see all the different designs people want and like.


Tee Print World Wide is located in Tampa Bay, Florida. Here we are able to get our hands on some quality workers and quality vinyl. The manufacturers that we get our supplies from are also here in Tampa. This means if we run out of vinyl we will still be able to complete your order same day.

Our delivery system is fairly simple. If you order from here in Tampa you will receive your order same day. If you live in the area you will receive your order in 1 day. If you live in Florida around a day to 2 days. Inside the states you could expect to recieve your package 2 days to 5 days depending on your location. Outside of the United States you could expect your order to be there from 7 days to 14 days depending on where you live.

Every package has a tracking number and a tracker placed inside the box. After your order you will recieve a tracking number, and after you go to the link given and put in your tracking number, and you are then able to watch your order.
